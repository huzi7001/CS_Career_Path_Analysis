## 1) AI, Machine Learning & Data Science
**Salary**
- Entry: **$40K–60K** (≈ PKR 200K–400K)
- Senior: **$100K+** (global roles, remote)

**Demand:** Exploding — driven by LLMs, healthcare AI, analytics, autonomous systems.

**Pros:** High income, cutting-edge; remote and freelance friendly.  
**Cons:** Steep math/stats curve; tools and best practices change fast.  
**Future:** Long-term growth; agentic systems and domain-specific AI will dominate.

---

## 2) Full-Stack Web Development
**Salary**
- Entry: **$20K–40K** (≈ PKR 100K–250K)
- Senior: **$70K+**

**Demand:** High — nearly every company needs web presence.

**Pros:** Easier to enter; abundant learning resources; many freelance gigs.  
**Cons:** Very competitive; junior tasks often repetitive.  
**Future:** Stable demand; low-level tasks increasingly automated by AI.

---

## 3) Mobile App Development (Flutter / React Native)
**Salary**
- **$25K–60K** (PKR 150K–350K+)

**Demand:** Moderate — strong in product-focused companies and startups.

**Pros:** High impact with small teams; strong freelance market.  
**Cons:** Store policies, platform fragmentation, UI bugs.  
**Future:** Solid, particularly for e-commerce, health, and education apps.

---

## 4) Cybersecurity / Ethical Hacking
**Salary**
- Entry: **$30K+**
- Certified Experts: **$80K–150K+**

**Demand:** Massive global shortage of talent.

**Pros:** Mission-critical, high respect, resilient to recessions.  
**Cons:** Highly technical; need continuous learning and certifications.  
**Future:** Urgent growth — security demand will only increase.

---

## 5) Cloud Computing & DevOps
**Salary**
- **$50K–130K+**

**Demand:** Very high — cloud-native architectures are standard.

**Pros:** High demand and broad industry adoption; critical for scalable products.  
**Cons:** Requires deep tooling knowledge (AWS/Azure/GCP, Docker, CI/CD).  
**Future:** Growing; combining DevOps with ML Ops will be especially valuable.

---

## 6) Blockchain / Web3
**Salary**
- **$50K–150K** (high variance)

**Demand:** Lower than peak; startups still hire niche talent.

**Pros:** Potentially huge upside with early startup roles.  
**Cons:** Risky market; fewer stable local opportunities.  
**Future:** Uncertain — long-term possibilities exist but short-term volatility.

---

## 7) Game Development
**Salary**
- **$25K–70K**

**Demand:** Moderate globally, smaller locally.

**Pros:** Creative work, passionate community (Unity, Unreal).  
**Cons:** Competitive and project-based; financial stability varies.  
**Future:** Steady; AR/VR will create niches.

---

## Final Verdict (practical ranking for 2025+)
1. **AI / ML / Data Science** — best for high pay + growth.
2. **Cloud + DevOps** — essential for modern infra and AI deployment.  
3. **Cybersecurity** — recession-proof and high demand.
4. **Web & Mobile Development** — great entry path + freelancing. 
5. **Blockchain / Gaming** — niche, high risk/reward.
